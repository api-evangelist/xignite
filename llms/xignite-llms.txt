# Xignite

> Xignite pioneered cloud-based financial market data APIs, offering a catalog of 40+ REST web services covering equities, forex, crypto, indices, options, futures, fixed income, mutual funds, corporate actions, news, ESG, and reference data. Each API lives on its own subdomain (for example globalquotes.xignite.com) and returns JSON, XML, or CSV, with bulk datasets delivered as files via CloudFiles and streaming quotes via its CloudStreaming product. Founded in 2000, Xignite was acquired by QUODD (a NewSpring Holdings company) in February 2023; the xignite.com developer catalog remains live while corporate pages redirect to quodd.com, and QUODD runs a newer developer platform at developer.quodd.com.

## APIs

- [Xignite Global Quotes API](https://www.xignite.com/product/global-stock-quote-data/): Delayed stock quotes for equities across global exchanges, served as REST operations (GetGlobalDelayedQuote, symbol changes, chart bars) in JSON, XML, or CSV from the globalquotes.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalquotes.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Real-Time API](https://www.xignite.com/product/global-real-time-stock-quote-data/): Real-time stock quote data for global equities via REST request/response operations on the globalrealtime.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalrealtime.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Historical API](https://www.xignite.com/product/historical-stock-prices/): End-of-day and historical equity prices including historical quote ranges, chart bars, adjustment factors, and cash dividend history from the globalhistorical.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalhistorical.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Currencies API](https://www.xignite.com/product/forex/): Real-time, historical, and average foreign exchange rates - one of Xignite's longest-running and most widely used APIs - served from the globalcurrencies.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalcurrencies.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Metals API](https://www.xignite.com/product/gold-metal/): Spot prices, bars, and historical rates for precious metals such as gold, silver, platinum, and palladium from the globalmetals.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalmetals.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Crypto API](https://www.xignite.com/product/cryptocurrency-api/): Cryptocurrency market data including quotes, chart bars, and historical prices for digital assets from the crypto.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://crypto.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Indices API](https://www.xignite.com/product/index-values/): Stock market index and benchmark values with chart bars and index metadata from the globalindices.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalindices.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Options API](https://www.xignite.com/product/global-option-price-data/): Option price data including quotes and chains for listed equity options from the globaloptions.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globaloptions.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Futures API](https://www.xignite.com/product/XigniteGlobalFutures/): Futures contract prices and metadata across commodity, index, and financial futures from the globalfutures.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalfutures.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Bonds API](https://www.xignite.com/product/bond-prices/): Bond price data for fixed income securities from the bonds.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://bonds.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Money Markets API](https://www.xignite.com/product/swaps-treasuries-data/): Money market, treasury, swap, and interbank rate data from the moneymarkets.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://moneymarkets.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Master API](https://www.xignite.com/product/global-security-master-data/): Global security master and reference data - symbology, listings, identifiers, and instrument metadata - from the globalmaster.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalmaster.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Corporate Actions API](https://www.xignite.com/product/XigniteGlobalCorporateActions/): Corporate action events such as dividends, splits, mergers, and symbol changes from the globalcorporateactions.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalcorporateactions.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global News API](https://www.xignite.com/product/company-financial-news/): Company financial news headlines, market summaries, and press release data from the globalnews.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalnews.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Earnings Calendar API](https://www.xignite.com/product/earnings-calendar-data/): Company earnings announcement dates and earnings calendar data from the earningscalendar.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://earningscalendar.xignite.com)
- [Xignite NAVs API](https://www.xignite.com/product/mutual-fund-NAVs/): Mutual fund net asset values (NAVs), adjustment factors, and fund pricing history from the navs.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://navs.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Fund Fundamentals API](https://www.xignite.com/product/global-fund-market-data/): Fund fundamental data - holdings, performance, and profile details for mutual funds and ETFs - from the globalfundfundamentals.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalfundfundamentals.xignite.com)
- [Xignite FactSet Fundamentals API](https://www.xignite.com/product/factset-fundamentals-financials/): Company fundamentals and financial statement data sourced from FactSet, served from the factsetfundamentals.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://factsetfundamentals.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global ESG API](https://www.xignite.com/product/esg-api/): Environmental, social, and governance (ESG) data for companies from the globalesg.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalesg.xignite.com)
- [Xignite Global Holidays API](https://www.xignite.com/product/exchange-hours-calendars/): Exchange trading hours, market holidays, and calendar data for global exchanges from the globalholidays.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://globalholidays.xignite.com)
- [Xignite CloudAlerts API](https://www.xignite.com/product/market-data-alerts/): Market event alerting - create and manage alerts on market data conditions - from the alerts.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://alerts.xignite.com)
- [Xignite CloudStreaming API](https://www.xignite.com/product/CloudStreaming/): Streaming product for pushing real-time quotes directly to applications. The product page is live but transport details (protocol, endpoints) are only documented behind registration; no public spec was found, so none is included here.
- [Xignite CloudFiles API](https://www.xignite.com/product/historical-equities-prices-file/): Bulk file delivery service behind Xignite's file products (historical equity prices, bonds, options, currencies, and corporate actions files), served from the cloudfiles.xignite.com service. (base URL: https://cloudfiles.xignite.com)
